Steak Pasta - A Single Guy's Take on a Family Favorite

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium ribeye steak
  • 1 lb of penne pasta
  • 24oz of plain tomato sauce
  • 8oz can of tomato paste
  • 3 large cloves of garlic
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • salt

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Begin heating a pot full of water - enough to cover the pasta
  2. While the water is getting up to a boil, slice the ribeye steak into pieces at about half an inch each
  3. Place the steak pieces into a ziploc bag that contains 2 tbsp of each seasoning. Shake vigorously and let sit
  4. Chop garlic cloves
  5. Once the water is at a rolling boil, add pasta until fully cooked
  6. Drain pasta, saving about a cup of pasta water, and drizzle olive oil on the so the pasta doesn't stick to each other. Place pasta in a separate bowl while we prepare the sauce
  7. Place the same pot on the stove to get ripping hot then add some olive oil and the chopped garlic. Cook the garlic until it's golden brown
  8. Add the steak pieces and allow the steak to get light brown, with some pieces crusting up a little bit. Just don't cook the pieces to well done
  9. Add the tomato paste and pasta water and stir until most of the water is cooked out of the sauce. Then add the remaining tomato sauce and stir until the sauce thickens
  10. Add the pasta into the pot of pasta sauce, add another tbsp of each seasoning to the sauce, stir and enjoy with some parmesan cheese on top
