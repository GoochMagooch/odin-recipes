Steak Pasta - A Single Guy's Take on a Family Favorite

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium ribeye steak
  • 1 lb of penne pasta
  • 1 jar of quality pasta sauce
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • salt

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Begin heating a pot full of water - enough to cover the pasta
  2. While the water is getting up to a boil, slice the ribeye steak into pieces at about half an inch each
  3. Place the steak pieces into a ziploc bag that contains 2 tablespoons of each seasoning. Shake vigorously and let sit
  4. Once the water is at a rolling boil, add pasta until fully cooked
  5. Drain pasta, saving about a cup of pasta water, and add olive oil so the pasta doesn't stick to each other. Place pasta in a separate bowl to sit while we prepare the sauce
  6. Place the same pot on the stove to get ripping hot then add the steak with a little bit of olive oil
  7. Allow the steak to get light brown, with some pieces crusting up a little bit. Just don't cook the pieces to well done
  8. Add pasta water & pasta sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes, or until the water has cooked out enough and the sauce is fairly thick
  9. Add the pasta into the pot of pasta sauce, add another tablespoon of each seasoning to the sauce, stir and enjoy with some parmesan cheese on top
