Brain Meal Recipe

This meal is a special meal, because it's a meal that is meant to nurture, and strengthen something I hold dear; my brain. The colene and omega 3's in this meal, when eaten regularly will turn your brain into a powerhouse of thinking and learning capability. Simple, but potent. As it should be

Ingredients:

  • One half of a whole salmon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Bring a pan to just above medium heat and drizzle avocado oil on it
  2. Lay the salmon skin side down. Leave for 5 minutes and then flip
  3. After the salmon has been on the second side for 5 minutes, crack all 3 eggs directly into the pan and let them cook
  4. Once the eggs have cooked all the way through, simply slide all of it onto a plate
  5. Place the spinach on the bottom of your bowl, drizzle the olive oil, set the fish and eggs of top of the spinach,cut up the avocado and place it on the fish and eggs. Enjoy...
  6. Your brain will thank you!
